RAPID - Ready Armor Protection for Instant Deployment

I am a co-inventor of the patented barrier system RAPID which stands for Ready Armor Protection for Instant Deployment. The RAPID barrier system is a blast, ballistic, and vehcile threat mitigation barrier that operates in urban environments. As an intern, I designed the first 3-D CAD models of the system and as a Research Engineer, I oversaw production of the first prototypes and then the final verison of the product. I led and assited in the blast, ballistic, and vehcile threat mitigation testing and published two technical reports and one submitted journal article with the research findings. U.S. Patent No. 10,775,138 A video with more information about the RAPID system can be found at the following link: RAPID system overview

Confidential Project #1

I led a project funded by the Army to design an unconvential protection system that obfuscated enemy intelligence. I first designed the system in the 3-D CAD software Solidworks and then lead production of the prototype. I machined all major componets of the protective system. We conducted research and experimentation with new finishing techniques so that the protection system could operate effectively in harsh environments.
